About Avg Run Club

FAQs

Do I need to be fast?

No — we run in pace groups. Everyone is welcome from walkers to marathoners.

What should I bring?

Comfortable shoes, water, and a light jacket if it's chilly. We recommend a small ID and phone.

Are dogs allowed?

On casual social runs yes — please keep them on a short leash and be mindful of others.
